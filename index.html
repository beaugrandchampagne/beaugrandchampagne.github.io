I was 18.


I Had gone through the worst abuse.


It seemed like there was no way out of the torment I was going through.


I had lost all hope.


It looked like everything was coming to an end for me.


But then, on a bright sunny day...


































































 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

We’d wake up by dawn, fall asleep by sunrise.


The days, the nights,

 we’d share them together,

light them together,

and savour them together.


It resulted into me experiencing things i thought would never be possible.


5000 nights and 5000 days later, after we last saw each other

this is what i want to say to you.


Blazing fire through the darkness…
 

Your calmness, happiness, and ambitiousness were contagious.
 

You could make a homeless person smile
or ease the pain of someone hurting badly.


You were a brick wall in front of the worst tsunami and tropical storms.


You were of the highest kindness.


You were inspiring, daring and brave.


You were a loyal friend and, a true teammate.
 

You were always impeccably groomed and smelled so good.
 

You were beautiful.

You were strong.
You were brilliant.
You were fierce.
You were manly.
You were valiant.
You were of trust.
You were one of a kind.


You had beautiful eyes…


You had an atomic heart.
 

In the day and in the night…you were incredible!


We’ve had so many wonderful moments together.
 

We would shine brighter than the day, and then, 

through the night, the moonlight would shine on us to highlight our greatness.


You were so kind to me.


You did better than accepting me for who I was—
you simply thought I was amazing, just like I thought you were amazing.


You never let me down.
 

You always made sure I was okay.


You showed me that I was worth something,
that I was a human,
that I deserved the best,
made me believe in myself,
lit up my soul,
sparked me to reach a level of success I wasn't even dreaming of,
Contributed to my understanding of how to have great exchanges with other beings,
and helped me become a man,


You showed me how to be brave, strong, balanced and resilient.


You made me become a champion.


You made my heart become an atomic heart.


Sage of the day, knight of the night…


You picked me first,
when everyone else had picked me last.
 

You showed interest in me,
when everyone else had shown none.


You wanted to be my friend,

when everyone else had rejected me.
 

You loved me,
when everyone else hated me.
 

You believed in me and knew I could become the greatest,
when everyone else had given up on me.


You comforted me through my biggest defeats
and cheered me on during my greatest victories. 


 light of my day,
peace of my night…


My precious dear…


You were my savior,
my hero,
my biggest inspiration,
my teammate,
my best friend,
my life partner,
my champion,
and my number one.


You may not exist as a being on Earth anymore,
but you will continue to exist in my memory,
in the love you left in me,
and in all the good that you did for this world.


I’m sure if you could thank me for giving you such a beautiful life you would.
Know that you, too, gave me a beautiful life.
 

I’m glad to have been your protector.
 

I enjoyed every single second we ever spent together.


You were the being that I loved the most in my life.
 

I will never forget everything that you’ve done for me.
 

No one will ever replace you.
 

Every Sunday, I will think of you while I eat my dinner.
 

I will always remember the bright sunny day that we met on.




























 




















 

"Every big victory, starts with small little steps."



























 

 

So, to all these nights and days that we’ve spent marveling at the world together…


It was a pleasure,

an honor,

and a privilege to have met you.
 

Thank you for everything you’ve given me.


When we met, 

you gave me a ticket to heaven, so here’s yours —you deserve it, Radiant Warrior.
